Our Mission & Vision

The Young Educator’s Society (YES Club) is committed to advancing Bachelor of Elementary Education (BEED) students by enhancing their knowledge, skills, and professional competence as future educators. Guided by the charism of St. Marcellin Champagnat and the ideals of a true Notre Damean, the organization fosters personal growth, camaraderie, service, solidarity, and teamwork among its members. It seeks to develop socially aware individuals who are responsive to community needs and capable of designing and implementing meaningful educational and outreach activities, ultimately shaping well-equipped, compassionate, and dedicated teachers for the future.

Goal

To bridge classroom theory with hands-on teaching practice.

Passion

Fostering a genuine love for mentorship and student growth.

Integrity

Upholding the highest standards of the teaching profession.
