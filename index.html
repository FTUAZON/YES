About

Be a Jack of All Trades and open doors to endless job opportunities. In schools, learning centers, NGOs, even abroad! With BEED, you’re not just trained to teach, you’re trained to adapt, lead, and inspire in any learning environment. The Bachelor of Elementary Education is your path to becoming a person with passion, love, and enthusiasm towards building a foundation for learners of society. With the aim to produce a fully equipped, goal-oriented, and a 21st century skilled elementary teachers, this program, together with the Young Educator's Society Club, will truly help in you grasping your personal goal towards reality. Choose BEED and be one of the YES Club family. Here, we learn to nurture young minds, to touch their hearts, and start to transform their lives.

Mission & Vision

Mission

The mission of The Young Educator’s Society (YES Club) is to enhance the knowledge and skills of its members to support their personal and professional growth, awaken their awareness of community needs, and encourage them to actively respond to these needs. It also aims to strengthen relationships among members by promoting camaraderie, service, solidarity, and teamwork among college students. Furthermore, the organization seeks to mold its members into competent and well-equipped future teachers with the necessary knowledge and expertise in the field of education. Ultimately, it endeavors to develop individuals who are not only aware of community concerns but are also capable of designing and implementing appropriate activities to address these needs.

Vision

To Advance the Bachelor of Elementary Education (BEED)students, to manifest the charism of St. Marcellin Champagnat and the ideals of true Notre Damean.
